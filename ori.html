Introduction

Gcinekyle (Pty) Ltd was established in 2020 after the Directors decided to combine their strengths in order to form a leading training company. With years of experience in the education and training industry and an experienced management team in place, our focus is on empowering organizations and developing a high powered individual and organizational skill set that will drive South Africa’s economy regionally and beyond borders.

We are a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor with our main office in Durban. Our team of qualified and industry experienced professionals are dedicated to providing the best training and development solutions to our clients.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to develop, build and service South Africa for living, working and travelling; in order to improve the quality of living for all.

Empowerment

Gcinekyle (Pty) Ltd has engaged the issue of empowerment boldly and decisively with solid business partners who have an active interest in our market. We have what it takes to ensure good governance and corporate management to cope with the increasing workload.

Core Values

At Gcinekyle (Pty) Ltd, we value Honesty, Integrity, Respect, Accountability, and Commitment. We are a company for the people, and we strive to uphold these values in all that we do.

Training and Development

Our learning interventions seek to narrow the performance gap by encouraging an environment of interactive education which provides learners the platform to achieve their full potential. Our full qualifications are aligned to the National Qualifications Framework, maximizing clients funding opportunities through Section 12 H (a) of the Income tax Act as well as Mandatory and Pivotal Grant reimbursement.

Disability Management

Gcinekyle (Pty) Ltd’s Disability Management Program provides employers a way to integrate and understand the employment of individuals with disabilities into their environment, creating an inclusive and productive culture within the workplace. Our in-house occupational therapists, expert mentors and coaches further ensure seamless disability integration within the workplace.
